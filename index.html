NEW ENGLISH SCHOOL, SAKRI

NEW ENGLISH HIGH SCHOOL was established in 1940 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in SAKRI block of DHULE district of Maharashtra.

The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Marathi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

1981 saw New English School being launched. The primary medium of instruction is Marathi with Others as secondary medium and the student teacher ratio is 25:1. The school takes pride of its excellent teaching methodology.The school provides education to students from class 6 to 12. This school's student strength is approximately 493. The school has given exceptional results in the academic sphere and its students have excelled in extra co-curricular activities too. 100% from this school qualified the examination.
